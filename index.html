About J.K.Enterprises

We are CPWD Registered contractors working since 1980 as one of the leaders in B&R Category. Being an active contractor for PAN India Govt. projects, we have executed major central & state PWD prestigious projects such as Office buildings, Hostel Blocks, Guest House, Residential Blocks, Hospitals & Education Buildings.


We excel in Internal & External Electrical installations, Street & Compound Lighting, Fire Fighting Works, Fire Alarm & PA SYSTEM, Water Supply & Drainage pumping system, DG Set, Sub Station Equipment's, UPS System, HVAC Works, Data Networking system, Surveillance & Access Control system, Audio Video System, STP & ETP, Solar Power Plant & Solar water Heating System, Civil Construction & Furnishing Works
